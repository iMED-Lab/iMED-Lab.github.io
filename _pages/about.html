Good day fellow readers and welcome to iMED Lab !

The iMED China (Intelligent Medical Imaging) medical imaging / ophthalmology imaging team takes "precision medicine" as the core direction, including a number of authoritative experts returning from overseas and outstanding domestic and foreign researchers. They have a multi-disciplinary research background and work experience in computer image processing, big data analysis, medical, optics, biomedical engineering, public health and statistics. Hope to combine the characteristics of Cixi Medical Institute of Ningbo Institute of Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and cooperate with international and domestic famous hospitals, international and domestic leading medical imaging instrument manufacturers, international and domestic scientific research institutions and university institutes with the same concept. Jointly build a world-leading "industry-university-research-use" integrated medical image processing center centered on the Cixi Medical Institute of the Ningbo Institute of Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
